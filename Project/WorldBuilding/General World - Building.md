Population involves a large human-merman split with some auxiliary species also present. Humans (30%), Merman (60%) Cecaelia, Siren, Centaurs, [[Auxillary Species]] etc. 

According to [[History]], the world is post-post-apocalyptic, with civilizations moved underwater to cope with increasing water levels and collapsing tectonic plates. This allows for interesting and historic remnants to be scattered around the maps. 

Common human supplements include webbed limbs, larger synthetic lungs, underwater visors, anti-moisturizers, propellors and underwater oxygen extractors.
