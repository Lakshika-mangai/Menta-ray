The MC you play is named Neel (they/them). They are an androgynous looking child around the age of 12-13. They are designed to be relatable to children of all genders. They are a silent protagonist. 

They are a merman-hybrid, able to breathe in both water and air. Neel lives in [[City - Thungabhadra]]

Neel carries a trident and a net. They have a companion mechanical seal. 

They work as a water scout tasked with helping out the people of their city, especially those who live in the outskirts of a city. They have several co-workers, such as [[Sahira]].
